My SMART Goals for This Year

  • Live and Adopt a Healthy Lifestyle

    Specific: Exercise for at least 30 minutes every day and eat a balanced diet.

    Measurable: Track progress using a fitness app and food journal.

    Achievable: Consult with a nutritionist and personal trainer to create a realistic plan.

    Relevant: Prioritize self-care to improve overall well-being and quality of life.

    Time-bound: Achieve target weight and fitness level by December 31st.

  • Graduate from my Bachelor of Health Science Degree

    Specific: Complete all required courses and assignments with a GPA of 3.5 or higher.

    Measurable: Monitor progress through course grades and academic advisor meetings.

    Achievable: Attend all classes regularly and seek help when needed from professors or tutors.

    Relevant: Pursue internships or volunteer opportunities related to my field of study to gain practical experience.

    Time-bound: Graduate with a Bachelor's degree by May 2024.

  • Buy a House

    Specific: Save a down payment of 20% of the desired home purchase price and secure pre-approval for a mortgage loan.

    Measurable: Track progress by regularly reviewing savings account balances and monitoring credit score changes.

    Achievable: Create a budget to prioritize saving for a down payment, reduce unnecessary expenses, and increase income through additional sources if feasible.

    Relevant: Research the real estate market to identify suitable properties within the desired location and price range, considering factors such as amenities, neighborhood, and future resale value.

    Time-bound: urchase a house within the next two years, ensuring financial readiness and stability before committing to a mortgage.
